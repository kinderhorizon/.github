# Kinder Horizon Foundation

Free, open-source communication tools for non-verbal children, anywhere in the world.

We are a registered British Columbia Society (S0085100, BN 72195 3636 BC0001) building tools for autistic children. Our first project is **Lighthouse AAC**, a privacy-first, on-device augmentative and alternative communication app. We are a husband-and-wife team of parents to an autistic child, and we build because we are tired of waiting for someone else to.

> Communication is a human right, not a premium feature.

## Open source plans

Lighthouse AAC lives in a private Flutter codebase today. We will publish it under MIT and Apache 2.0 once the closed alpha lands. We do not believe a communication tool for children can be a black box.

## How to help

- **Donate.** Live at [kinderhorizon.org/donate](https://kinderhorizon.org/donate) via Stripe (one-time, with preset tiers and custom amounts) and Zeffy (one-time and monthly, zero-fee). Contributions are not tax-deductible.
- **Translate.** Seven locales ship today (en, ar, es, fr-CA, de, pt-BR, zh-Hans). None have had a native-speaker review yet. Email if you can help.
- **Test, when alpha lands.** We are looking for families with non-verbal children to help shape Lighthouse AAC during closed alpha. Email if interested.
